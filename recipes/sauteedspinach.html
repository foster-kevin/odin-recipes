Sautéed Spinach

Description

Sautéed spinach cooked with garlic, butter and oil. A simple, delicious recipe requiring only a few minutes and a few ingredients. It makes for a great side dish, but it's' a good choice for a healthy snack too!

Ingredients

  • 2 large bunches spinach, about 1 pound
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, sliced
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Clean and prep the spinach
  2. Sauté the oil and garlic on medium-high heat for about 30 seconds
  3. Add the spinach to the pan and cook (covered), 1 minute each side
  4. Remove from heat and drain excess liquid. Sprinkle with salt to taste, serve immediately.
