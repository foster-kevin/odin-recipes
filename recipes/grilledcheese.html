Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

It doesn't get much simpler than a grilled cheese sandwich. 3 common ingredients and 5-10 minutes is all you need.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 1.5 tablespoons butter
  • 1 slice (or 2 slices) cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat. Spread butter on both sides of bread slices. Put cheese in between bread slices and place on skillet.
  2. Cook until golden brown on one side. Flip sandwich with a spatula and cook until cheese is melted and both sides are golden brown.
