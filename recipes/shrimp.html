Shrimp Pasta Alfredo

Description

Shrimp, pasta, and alfredo sauce. What's not to love about it? All you need is those three things, a Dutch oven (I don't even know what that is, but you might have one), and this recipe!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups uncooked bow tie pasta
  • 1 jar (15 ounces) alfredo sauce
  • 1 pound peeled and cooked shrimp, tails removed
  • (Optional) 2 cups frozen peas
  • (Optional) 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a Dutch oven, cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. (Optional) Add peas during the last 3 minutes of cooking.
  3. Drain water and return to pan.
  4. Stir in shrimp and sauce, heat through over medium heat, stirring occasionally.
  5. (Optional) Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
